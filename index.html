About Me

Hi, I’m Grace, a frontend web developer with a passion for building intuitive, responsive, and engaging user interfaces. With a year of experience in crafting websites and web applications, I specialize in turning ideas into dynamic, user-friendly digital experiences. My goal is to create beautiful and functional websites that not only look great but also provide seamless interactions for users.

My journey into web development began when I discovered the power of coding to bring designs to life. Starting from basic HTML and CSS, I’ve since expanded my expertise to include modern technologies like JavaScript, React, and CSS frameworks such as Bootstrap. I love staying on top of the latest frontend trends, whether it's experimenting with new tools or optimizing performance for a faster, more engaging user experience.

I’m skilled in building responsive websites that work across all devices, ensuring smooth and consistent functionality. From working with APIs to implementing complex animations, I thrive on projects that challenge my creativity and technical expertise.

Outside of coding, I’m passionate about exploring new technologies. This interest helps me stay inspired and push the boundaries of what’s possible in frontend development. I believe that continuous learning is key to staying ahead in the fast-evolving world of web development.

I’m always eager to collaborate on exciting projects or discuss new ideas. Whether you're looking to build a sleek new website or improve an existing one, feel free to reach out—I’d love to help bring your vision to life!
